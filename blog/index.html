July 05, 2012

Rose Guns Days Demo Translated!

It's been a long time, but the Witch Hunt is finally back, and looking forward to a fresh start. In Rose Guns Days, 07th Expansion is starting up on an entirely new genre: adventure, alternate history, and a small, yet oddly satisfying new feature that none of us saw coming.

The Japanese demo for this new series was released on July 6th, and we're happy to be offering a complete translation today, along with the usual Windows and Mac installers. Files and instructions can be found on our website. 07th Expansion has also given us permission to host the Japanese game files from our own US and EU-friendly servers, so the download will hopefully be a bit faster for those of us not living in Japan at the moment. Special thanks to everyone who helped provide this hosting, including Roto, Message, and teus.

Unlike the past 07th Expansion series, this demo is only part of the first game and runs 2-3 hours for most players. It's just a little taste of what Ryuukishi has planned for the next few years, but it's got us fired up to jump back into translating.

We plan to translate the full Japanese game, but as usual, that project will begin after the Japanese game's release, so we ask that you bear with us as we catch up to the original series. A huge thanks to all of our text and picture editors, and everyone who helped get the installers ready on such short notice.

EP8 Completed!

At long last, we've reached the end of this long journey. The final section of Umineko no Naku Koro Ni is now avaliable in English. You can find the complete translation of Umineko Chiru on our Getting Started page. As usual, I'd like to thank all of the many editors and fans who have stuck with us so long. It's rare to find a series that keeps people as long as Umineko, and we certainly wouldn't have been able to keep this up without your support and encouragement. I apologize for the delay in getting this last patch to you, the fans, but I hope you enjoy the conclusion of this series.

May 09, 2011

Episode 8: Part 1 Released

After a long delay, we're finally releasing a patch that works with Episode 8. Our apologies to everyone we kept waiting.  Part 1 covers roughly the first 50% of the game, but it was by far the hardest thing we've ever had to translate.  It was a challenge for us, and it'll probably be a challenge for you.

 As usual, your old save files will not be usable when you update to the full patch (coming soon).  However, we will create a way to manually unlock the chapter select for Part 1.  This unlock can be activated at any time, whether you've updated from the old patch or installed it on a new computer.

 Hopefully, the download version of EP8 will be coming soon, but probably not for at least a week.

 As always, the patch can be found here: http://www.witch-hunt.com/stage.html
