PROJECT INFO

Witch Hunt is a fan translation group working on
the latest installment in the When They Cry series
created by 07th Expansion.  Head over to the
Getting Started page to learn how to buy the
game and download our patch.

No patch files linked to on this site work
without a purchased copy of the game!

We are definitely not here to help people steal this
great game, so all of our patches require a full
version of the game to run.  Fortunately,
Umineko is a doujin game and fairly cheap, so
support the incredible people who made it and
buy a copy!
